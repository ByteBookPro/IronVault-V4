## Full Description
IronVault is a military-grade password manager that keeps your credentials, notes, documents, and financial data locked behind AES-256 encryption.

**Features:**
• Store unlimited passwords, notes, and secure documents
• AES-256-GCM encryption — your data never leaves your device unencrypted
• Multi-vault support — separate personal and work identities
• Subscription tracker — never miss a renewal
• Expense tracker with multi-currency support
• API key vault for developers
• Biometric unlock (Face ID / Fingerprint)
• Auto-lock and failed-attempt protection
• Offline-first — works without internet

**Privacy First:**
IronVault uses end-to-end encryption. We cannot see your data. Ever.
